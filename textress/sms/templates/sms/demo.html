It starts with the Event

A Business Event at your Hotel initiates the transaction. For example, a guest has tried to check in early, but the room is not clean yet. At this step the housekeeping attendant has cleaned the room.

The Hotel Sytem receives an update.

The Hotel System is updated as normal procedure that the room is now clean via the house keeping attendant punching a code into the phone in the hotel room for example.


Textress is alerted of the Business Event

Textress is notified through a Secure Interface with the Hotel System of the Business Event, and will now communicate the information to the Hotel Guest.

The Message is delivered to the Hotel Guest

The Hotel guest receives an SMS message that their room is now clean. This message is secure, and can be personally tailored to the Guest and to fit your Brand.
