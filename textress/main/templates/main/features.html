Our Features

Hotels

Clean room notifications. Front desk agents no longer need to call each individual guest. A simple alert is sent out automatically when the room is clean.

Restaurants

Let customers spend money on property instead of waiting in line. Send a pre-alert when their table is close to being ready, so the customer has enough time to claim their table.

Personal

Call customers by name or any other personal touches that your business would like to add when sending text messages.

API Integration

Integrate our text messaging solutions with existing systems. Specify how software components should work together. Share data accross applications.

Development

All local U.S. based development. We guarantee a quick turnaround and truly understanding your business needs. We pride ourselves on thoughtful solutions, which begin with development.

Analytics

If a Key Volume Indicator (KVI) touches our system, we can add data analytics around it. See KVIs accross different time periods combined with different dimensions to better understand business volumes.

Customer Centric

Only the most important messages. The goal is not to spam customers. Customers can opt out at any time.

Data Collection

An additional data collection point to add to your marketing efforts. Phone numbers and emails can be saved with the customers' permission, and used for marketing communications at a later date.
