Guest Service

The platfom is designed with Guest Service as the number one objective.

When designing the Textress SMS Service, we consistently asked, "How can we improve Guest Service, and what features are important?"

Confirm


Ask Guests to respond "Y" to Confirm and their Phone Number will be auto-confirmed in the system.

Opt-out


The same for opting out. Guest can respond "S" at any time to opt-out and will be auto-unsubscribed from SMS messaging.

Help


Text "H" any time for "Help". Use the default Help message or custom set your Help message for your hotel.

Customizable

Confirm, Opt-out, welcome and thank you for staying messages can be turned on and off as well as you can set your own auto responses.

Welcome Message

Send a Welcome message the Hotel Guest. They will now have the SMS Concierge phone number.

Thank You for Staying

Thank Guests for staying. Even send them a post-stay survey link.

SMS Signatures

Use a global SMS signature for your Hotel, or have personal SMS signatures for your employees.

Create Your Own Auto Responses

Choose the letter and the auto-response that you want.

Asynchronous

All messages show on screen as soon as they are sent.

Incoming and Outgoing

All incoming and outgoing messages show asynchonously and are saved for data reporting.

Alert Bar

The interface shows incoming message alerts for SMS messages that haven't been read yet.

Messages Statuses

Once messages are viewed their status will be updated to "read".

Phone Number

Your Hotel's SMS Phone Number will be local to your area code.

User Permissions

There are 3 permission levels. Admin, Manager, and Employee. With different levels of access for the structure of your hotel operation.

Business Intelligence

It's your data! You can access raw data or charted data for all SMS business events.

Data

All raw Data is available for Messages, Guests, and Users.

Charts

Charted data is available for usage by User, Guest, date range, etc... Slice and dice your data, and make a cool chart.

Printable PDFs

Raw data outputs to CSV, and Charts output to PDF.
